About me

Hi, I'm Adrián de Jesús Fernández, a multidisciplinary graphic designer with a focus on the intersection of visual storytelling and interactive design. My professional practice is defined by the creation of complex visual systems, where every aesthetic decision is backed by functional logic.

Design with intention. Multidisciplinary thinking.

Currently, I am enriching my knowledge through a Master in Interactive Design (UX/UI) at ESDMadrid. My goal is to provide creative teams with a comprehensive vision: the ability to unite the rigor of editorial and brand graphic design with the innovation and usability of digital experiences.

I bring seriousness to each project, technical commitment and constant curiosity. I seek to be part of processes that challenge creative limits and that focus on memorable and purposeful design solutions.
